Let's Play Rock Paper Scissors

Rules:

In this online rock-paper-scissors game, players engage in a classic duel against a computer opponent, testing their strategic prowess and luck. The rules are simple: players must click on their chosen symbol - rock, paper, or scissors - to initiate a round. The computer then randomly selects its own move. The outcome of the round is determined by the familiar rules: rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock.
